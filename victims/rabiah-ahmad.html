Rabiah Ahmad - July 31, 2020

Police continue to seek information regarding the homicide of Rabiah Ahmad, 30, and her baby Ahja on July 31, 2020. Rabiah was 28 weeks pregnant when she was shot while inside a residence in the 6600 block of Dovecote Drive in Columbia. She later died at the hospital. Doctors delivered Ahja, who remained in critical condition until she died Aug. 5. Detectives do not believe that Ahmad was targeted and are still working to determine the motive in the shooting.
