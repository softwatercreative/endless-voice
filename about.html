About Endless Voice

There’s a disturbing gap in the digital landscape when it comes to the unresolved cases in Howard County. Today, information on these cold cases is buried in outdated government websites or scattered across sketchy web pages cluttered with unrelated ads. As a result, the victims—whose stories deserve to be told—have virtually no web presence, and the cases themselves receive only sporadic coverage from local outlets like ABC or WBAL. Maryland needs a dedicated platform to amplify the voices of the victims and bring their stories into the light.

Our Mission

We are committed to transforming the way cold cases are approached in Howard County by creating a modern, centralized resource that highlights the victims, documents each case, and provides a secure channel for the community to offer tips. Our goal is to honor the memory of the victims and galvanize public support for renewed investigative efforts.

Our Solutions

Centralized Website: We are building a comprehensive home website for Howard County Cold Cases. This modern platform will serve as a reliable resource, presenting detailed information on each case alongside ways for the public to submit tips. Dedicated Victim Pages: Each of the 26 victims will have a dedicated web page, complete with unique links that are easily shareable across various online platforms. This ensures every story can gain the visibility it deserves and potentially reach a wider audience. Fundraising for Sustainability: To keep this resource accessible and free from the start, we will launch fundraising campaigns to cover the initial setup and future hosting costs. Your support is vital in sustaining this platform. Community Connection: We aim to connect with the wider Crime Solvers Community, inviting collaboration and collective action. Together, we can foster an environment where every case is actively revisited and every tip is valued. Join us in our mission to create a space where every voice is heard, every case is spotlighted, and every effort to bring justice is amplified. Your support helps ensure that no victim is forgotten and that the pursuit of truth continues with unwavering resolve.
